## 3. Revenue Strategy Execution

**Approach: Free Demo → Paid Conversion Model**

1. Identify businesses with weak or no online presence.
2. Contact decision-makers via WhatsApp, email, or phone.
3. Understand their business challenges.
4. Offer a free consultation/demo.
5. Showcase potential solutions and expected business benefits.
6. Transfer interested clients to the company team for pricing and project discussions.
7. Follow up after the demo to address questions.

**Expected Outcome:**
The free demo reduces risk for the client and increases trust, improving conversion rates.
